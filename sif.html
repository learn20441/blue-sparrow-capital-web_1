What is Structured Investment Fund (SIF)?

Structured Investment Funds (SIFs) are advanced investment products that combine multiple financial instruments such as bonds, equities, derivatives, and other assets into a single portfolio. They are designed to offer customized risk-return profiles that align with an investor's specific goals.

Why Invest in SIF?

  • Tailored investment strategies based on your risk appetite.
  • Potential for higher returns through diversified asset allocation.
  • Professional management by experienced fund managers.
  • Access to both domestic and international markets.
