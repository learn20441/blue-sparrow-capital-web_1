Types of Insurance We Offer

Five categories with plan types and riders. Purely informational.

Plan Types

  • Term: high cover, low premium; no maturity value.
  • Savings/Traditional: guaranteed benefits & survival payouts.
  • ULIP: market-linked investment + insurance; switch funds.

Riders & What They Do

  • Accidental Death Benefit — extra payout on accidental death
  • Accidental Permanent Disability — payout/waiver on disability
  • Critical Illness — lump sum on listed CI
  • Waiver of Premium — future premiums waived on CI/disability
  • Income Benefit — nominee gets monthly income

Plan Types

  • Individual Health — separate sum insured per person
  • Family Floater — shared sum insured for family
  • Senior Citizen Plans — higher coverage, disease-specific benefits
  • Top-Up / Super Top-Up — extra cover beyond deductible
  • Critical Illness — one-time payout on diagnosis

Popular Add-ons

  • Room Rent Waiver/Upgrade
  • NCB Booster
  • Restore/Refill Benefit
  • OPD / Day-care / Maternity
  • Co-pay Waiver & Hospital Cash

Types of Plans

  • Third-Party (TP) Only — legal compliance; no own damage
  • Own Damage (OD) Only — your vehicle damage; TP separate
  • Comprehensive (TP + OD) — complete protection

Add-ons & What They Do

  • Zero Depreciation — higher claim payout on parts
  • Engine & Gearbox Protect — water/engine damages
  • Return to Invoice — invoice value in total loss/theft
  • NCB Protect — retain NCB after claim (conditions)
  • RSA, Consumables, Key/Lock, Tyre/Rim, PA cover

Plan Types

  • Single Trip / Multi-Trip Annual
  • Student Travel (long-stay)
  • Senior/Business/Luxury variants

Add-ons

  • Adventure Sports Cover
  • Trip Cancellation/Interruption
  • Pre-Existing Disease extension (if available)
  • Gadget Cover / Baggage Delay / Home Burglary

Core Covers

  • Shop/Office Package
  • Fire & Allied Perils (SFSP) + Burglary
  • Marine Transit (Inland/Import/Export)
  • Liability (Public/Product) & Professional Indemnity
  • Workmen/Employee Compensation, D&O
  • Group Health/PA

Add-ons

  • Business Interruption (Loss of Profit)
  • RSMD & STFI, Earthquake/Terrorism
  • Fidelity Guarantee, Money Insurance
  • Electronic Equipment/Breakdown
